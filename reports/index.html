NearlyKilled.me Reports

NearlyKilledMe reports are created once per week, except when the author is traveling without internet access. These reports contain a summary of data from the past 7 days, including selected excerpts from reports, and various statistics.

All data is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 License, meaning you are free to use the data from the reports as long as you give us credit and allow others to use your work in turn.
