NearlyKilledMe is a project to raise awareness of driver inattention and willful violation of basic traffic laws. Traffic deaths in Oregon are already up more than 40% over last year, and many are demanding that something be done.

Industrial facilities must report "near-miss accidents", but the hundreds of near misses on our state's roads every day go unreported and unrecognized. This is our chance to speak up.

Submissions to this site will be sent to PBOT, ODOT, and PPB every week. Please report every near-miss and every dangerous driver action you see, so that our governments can see the true impacts from lax enforcement of dangerous driving.
